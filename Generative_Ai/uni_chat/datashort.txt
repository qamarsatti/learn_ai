A computer is an electronic device that is capable of processing data according to instructions provided by a user or a program It can perform a variety of operations such as data storage data retrieval data processing and communication Modern computers typically consist of a central processing unit CPU memory inputoutput devices such as a keyboard and mouse and storage devices such as a hard drive or solidstate drive They can be used for a wide range of tasks from simple calculations and word processing to complex scientific simulations and artificial intelligence applications Computers have revolutionized many fields including business education healthcare and entertainment and have become an essential tool for people around the world
